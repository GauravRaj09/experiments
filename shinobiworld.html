ITACHI UCHIHA


Itachi Uchiha (うちはイタチ) was a shinobi of Konohagakure's Uchiha clan who served as an Anbu Captain.

OBITO UCHIHA


Obito Uchiha (うちはオビト, Uchiha Obito) was a member of Konohagakure's Uchiha clan.

MADARA UCHIHA


Madara Uchiha (うちはマダラ, Uchiha Madara) was the legendary leader of the Uchiha Clan. He founded Konohagakure Village.

NARUTO UZUMAKI


Naruto Uzumaki (うずまきナルト) is a shinobi of Konohagakure. He became the jinchūriki of the Nine-Tails on the day of his birth.

BORUTO UZUMAKI


Boruto Uzumaki (うずまきボルト) is a missing-nin from Konohagakure's Uzumaki clan and a direct descendant of the Hyūga clan through his mother.

NAGATO (PAIN)


Nagato (長門) was a shinobi of Amegakure and descendant of the Uzumaki clan. Formed Akatsuki with his friends Yahiko and Konan.

HASHIRAMA SENJU


Hashirama Senju (千手柱間) was a member of the famed Senju clan. He himself was hailed as the "God of Shinobi" for his unmatched ninja prowess.

TOBIRAMA SENJU


Tobirama Senju (千手扉間) was a member of the renowned Senju clan, who, together with his elder brother and the Uchiha clan, founded the first shinobi village: Konohagakure.

TSUNADE


Tsunade (綱手) is a descendant of the Senju and Uzumaki Clan, and is one of Konohagakure's Sannin. She is famed as the world's strongest kunoichi and its greatest medical-nin
